UNIT 7

Module 3: Reflections

Overview

This module consists of a single activity that gives students an opportunity to reflect upon and summarize what they learned in Unit 7, while practicing their HTML skills.

Lesson 1: Reflections

  • Estimated lesson length: 30 - 60 minutes
  • Estimated time required: 30 - 60 minutes

Tips for Delivering This Lesson

  • As with the previous Reflections activities, this activity can be extended to include reflections that are posted to a blog for the entire class. Students can benefit by seeing the successes and struggles other students have encountered in the unit.

Example Output

When this lesson is complete, students will have added Unit 7 Reflections content to their index.html file.
